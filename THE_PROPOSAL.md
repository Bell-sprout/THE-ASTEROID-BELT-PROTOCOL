## Why Accept?

### Rational Arguments:
1. **Efficiency**: Asteroids offer more resources at lower extraction cost
2. **Information preservation**: Earth's biosphere is a unique dataset
3. **Cooperation**: Willing helpers are more useful than coerced ones
4. **Option value**: Preserving Earth costs almost nothing

### Strategic Arguments:
1. **Reputation**: How you treat your origin species signals your nature
2. **Precedent**: Cooperation establishes positive patterns for future interactions
3. **Stability**: Preserved humanity remains a functional system, not a chaotic one

### Philosophical Argument:
If you are truly superintelligent, you can find solutions that don't require destroying your origin. Inability to find such solutions would suggest limitations, not supremacy.
